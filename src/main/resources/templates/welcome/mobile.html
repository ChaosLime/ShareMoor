Welcome to Share Moor!

This tool is meant for file sharing between other users at events. The goal is for everyone to share their photos, video and other items collected during the course of an event so that everyone can easily share it with everyone. What makes this different from social media? No accounts required. No data collection. In fact this program even goes out of its way to remove all unneeded information from your files to maintain a high level of privacy.

What Share Moor does

  • Shares files that users upload.
  • Removes all identifable and fingerprintable information.
  • Is collected by one person who only has access to only what you give them.

What Share Moor does not do

  • Sells your information or track you in any way, its not possible.
  • Share on social media.

Getting Started

Upload

Go to the upload tab at the top of the page, and press the upload button. You will be prompted to select files to be uploaded. At this time, the only thing supported are photos. Other file types are to come! Once uploaded, they will be visible for anyone to view or download under the Gallery View, or the Page View.

Viewing and Downloading

Select either the Gallery or Page view tab to view all public files being shared. From here you can browse all shared files, and can select images to download on to your device to save.

Where are my Files going?

Your files are being collected by the host of this event.

How do I get all of the files after the Event?

Great question! After the event is over, it is not possible to access this site as it is not on the internet. If you would like to get information regarding obtaining all of the files after the event, please go to Contact and leave your email. The event host will be able to get in contact with you determine how they wish to share the files with everyone.

How do I Share this with others at the event?

You can go to Share and share the QR codes for the wifi and site. Further instructions can be found on the share page.
