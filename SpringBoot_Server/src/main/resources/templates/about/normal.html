About

Share Moor is a file sharing platform intended events were individuals who want to find an easy way to share their files such as photos or videos in a fast, easy, and reliable manner that also respects their privacy. This program is no more than a tool that allows individuals to share their files with a group at an event, and those files are then collected by the person or group hosting this program.

Created By

Mitchell and Nicholas Saunders. Brothers who felt the need of many who have been frustrated by the world of technology and just wanted to use something simple that just worked.
